# Home — Midnight Signal override

This file overrides `design-system/MASTER.md` for the Home page only.

## Purpose

Introduce Byulmaru and its product through one memorable authored scene, then return to clear editorial chapters. The hero may be visually rich; the rest of the page must provide contrast through restraint.

## Hero: narrative space scene

- The scene is a composed story, not a night-sky background.
- Combine four layers: restrained print-like cosmic texture, one central authored illustration, a small number of real content-bearing UI/editorial fragments, and the hero copy/CTA.
- The central illustration is the anchor. Panels explain or extend its story; they are not generic floating dashboard cards.
- Use Acid for the primary action, Cyan for product/information cues, and Coral for human/editorial details. Never blend all three into a rainbow gradient.
- Keep the canvas `#15111F`. Texture may shift value within the dark family but must not introduce a purple haze behind every element.
- Stars, if any, are sparse incidental marks within the illustration texture. Do not render a procedural star field or astronomy wallpaper.
- One controlled spotlight or beam is allowed when it belongs to the illustration. Prefer a flat color-to-transparent mask; do not use a multicolor aurora gradient.

## Composition

- Desktop may use overlap and asymmetry at variance `7/10`, but headline, CTA, and navigation remain unobstructed.
- Floating fragments must contain real copy, product evidence, or a meaningful story step.
- Do not imitate Sentry's astronaut, UFO, monitoring panels, or exact composition. Use the reference only for the principle of integrated narrative staging.
- On mobile, linearize as copy → illustration → evidence fragments. Remove decorative overlap before reducing readable type.

## Following chapters

- Do not repeat the cosmic scene, particles, large illustration, or ambient glow.
- Use editorial split layouts, ruled statements, member/product imagery, and restrained surfaces.
- Values do not default to three equal neon-outline cards. Choose a list, staggered statements, or content-led modules based on final copy.
- One signal color should dominate each chapter; other colors appear only as small cross-references.

## Motion

- Optional hero entrance: copy and illustration settle within `300ms`; evidence fragments may follow once with `40–60ms` stagger.
- No looping drift, twinkling stars, parallax, mouse-following glow, or perpetual panel bobbing by default.
- Reduced motion shows the final composition immediately.
