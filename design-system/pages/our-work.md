# Our Work — Midnight Signal override

This file overrides `design-system/MASTER.md` for the Our Work page only.

## Purpose

Explain one social product and support a future pre-registration journey. Product evidence and understandable benefits take priority over spectacle.

## Color

- Cyan is the dominant signal for product information, navigation state, diagrams, and focus.
- Acid is reserved for the primary conversion action and confirmed positive states.
- Coral marks creator or community stories, not errors or system status.

## Product representation

- Use screenshots, honest prototypes, diagrams, or clearly labeled conceptual previews.
- Do not turn the product into a terminal, observability dashboard, code editor, AI chat shell, or sci-fi HUD.
- Fake controls and fabricated metrics are prohibited. A conceptual panel must be labeled or framed so it cannot be mistaken for shipped functionality.
- Dense technical material may use tabular numbers but retains Pretendard; no monospace visual theme.

## Layout

- Keep persistent tab/navigation behavior usable and visually quieter than the page heading.
- Use progressive disclosure for architecture and operational detail; overview content remains readable without interaction.
- Prefer connected sequences, annotated screenshots, and comparison tables over a repeated bento grid.
- Reserve the strongest visual scene for one product proposition. Other sections return to the base canvas and ruled editorial structure.

## Effects and motion

- Product diagrams may use a limited Cyan signal path or one-time connection animation when it clarifies flow.
- No decorative data streams, scanning lines, particle networks, or continuous dashboard animation by default.
- Reduced motion preserves the complete diagram and state labels without animation.
