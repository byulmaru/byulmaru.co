# About Us — Midnight Signal override

This file overrides `design-system/MASTER.md` for the About Us page only.

## Purpose

Present the people, working principles, and team context with an editorial human tone. This page is not a second product landing page.

## Color and imagery

- Coral is the page's dominant signal for people, quotations, and editorial emphasis.
- Acid remains reserved for the primary action; Cyan identifies product or technical references only.
- Prefer real member photography or authored portraits with consistent crops. Do not replace missing imagery with AI-generated headshots or abstract avatars.

## Layout

- Use generous type-led introductions, ruled biographies, and asymmetric image/text pairs.
- Member modules may float or overlap only when an explicit art direction calls for a collage; each module must still carry a name, role, and accessible reading order.
- Avoid three identical role cards as the default representation of planning, design, and development.
- Quotes need a real speaker and context. Do not invent testimonial copy to fill space.

## Effects

- No galaxy scene, star field, ambient blob, or neon portrait glow by default.
- Print texture or a single hard-edged color crop may support the editorial tone without becoming a full-page effect.
